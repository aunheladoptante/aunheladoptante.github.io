Current Focus • Lead Researcher

My primary research involves benchmarking and optimizing Open-Source Large Language Models (LLMs) for the Philippine context. I focus on Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) systems that can navigate the linguistic complexities of Taglish (Tagalog-English code-switching), ensuring that AI-driven knowledge retrieval remains accurate and culturally nuanced for local organizations.

I contributed extensively to the iTANONG ecosystem, focusing on Text-to-SQL semantic parsing.
